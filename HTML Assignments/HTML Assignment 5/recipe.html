Recipe of Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:-

  1. 200g spaghetti
  2. 4 cloves garlic (sliced)
  3. 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  5. Salt (to taste)
  6. Fresh parsley (chopped)
  7. Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions:-

  • Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions.
  • While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until the garlic turns golden brown (2-3 minutes).
  • Drain the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  • Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan, tossing it in the garlic oil. If needed, add a little reserved pasta water for extra moisture.
  • Season with salt, sprinkle with chopped parsley, and top with Parmesan if desired.

Recipe of Chow Mein

Ingredients:-

  1. 200g chow mein noodles (or any thin noodles)
  2. 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. 1/2 cup sliced onions
  4. 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  5. 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  6. 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  7. 2 tbsp soy sauce
  8. 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  9. 1/2 tsp sugar
  10. Salt and pepper (to taste)
  11. Green onions (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions:-

  • Cook the chow mein noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add onions, bell peppers, and carrots, and sauté for 3-4 minutes.
  • Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  • Add the cooked noodles to the pan, then stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sugar. Toss everything together until well coated.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste, and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.

Biryani

Ingredients:-

  1. 1 cup basmati rice
  2. 500g chicken (or beef, lamb, or vegetables)
  3. 1 large onion (sliced), 2 tomatoes (chopped), 2 tbsp yogurt
  4. 2 tbsp biryani masala, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp garam masala
  5. 2 tbsp ghee (or vegetable oil)
  6. 3 cups water, 2 bay leaves, 4 cloves
  7. 2 cardamom pods
  8. Fresh cilantro and mint (for garnish)

Instructions:-

  • Rinse the basmati rice and soak it for 30 minutes. Set aside.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom, and bay leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, biryani masala, and garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Stir in chicken (or other meat), cook until browned, then add yogurt. Cook until meat is tender.
  • In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and add salt. Add the soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain the water.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat, cover, and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes (dum cooking).
